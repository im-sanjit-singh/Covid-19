Prevention From Corona Virus

Stay Home, Stay Safe

HOW TO PREVENT VIRUS

Wash Your Place

Sanitize your surrounding.

Maintain Distance

Avoid close contact with anyone with cold or flu-like symptoms.

Don't Touch Face

Avoid Touching eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.

Wash Your Hand

Wash you hand regularly with soap and water or alcohol based hand rub.

Use Napkin

Catch coughs and sneezes with napkins or disposable tissues.

Wear a Mask

Always wear a mask to prevent droplets from going outside.
